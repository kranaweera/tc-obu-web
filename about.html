About Us

The story of Thurstan College OBU Australia — our mission, values, and journey

Who We Are

Our Story

The Thurstan College Old Boys' Union (OBU) Australia is a vibrant community of former students of Thurstan College, Colombo, Sri Lanka — now proudly based across Australia.

We are united by our shared heritage, school values, and a commitment to fostering fellowship among old boys while supporting the ongoing development of our beloved alma mater.

Our union organises cultural events, social gatherings, and fundraising initiatives to give back to Thurstan College and strengthen the bonds between old boys in Australia.

Our Journey

Our History

1997

OBU Australia Founded

Formation of the Thurstan College Old Boys' Union Australia, officially established and uniting Thurstanites across the continent.

1997

Inaugural Dinner Dance

The very first OBU Australia Dinner Dance — a landmark evening that established the tradition of annual celebrations bringing the community together.

2010

The Gypsies Dance

A vibrant and colourful themed evening celebrating the spirit and culture of OBU Australia, drawing members from across the country.

2013

Stars of the 70s Dinner Dance

A nostalgic tribute to the golden era of the 1970s, bringing Thurstanites together in style to relive fond memories of the decade.

2023

Night of the Knights in Winter Wonderland

An enchanting winter-themed gala celebrating the bonds of brotherhood and fellowship among Thurstanites in Australia.

2025

75th Anniversary Gala Dinner Dance — Alakamandawa

The grand 75th Anniversary celebration held in Melbourne, bringing together Thurstanites from across Australia for an unforgettable evening marking a historic milestone.

We are actively compiling our history. If you have stories, photos, or memories from OBU Australia events over the years, please get in touch — we'd love to hear from you.
